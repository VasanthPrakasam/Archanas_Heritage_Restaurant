Our Story

A Heritage of Flavour, Served with Heart

At Archanas Heritage, every dish is a celebration of culture and culinary tradition. From the bold, peppery notes of authentic Chettinadu chicken to delicate vegetarian platters, our kitchen brings together the rich tapestry of Indian multi-cuisine under one roof.

Nestled in the serene surroundings of Kollapatty, our restaurant offers a clean, neat, and family-friendly atmosphere that regulars and travellers alike return to again and again. We believe great food should be accessible — that's why we keep our prices honest and our portions generous.
